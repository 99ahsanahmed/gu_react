About US

Foodpanda is a popular online food delivery platform that connects users with a wide range of local restaurants and eateries. Launched in 2012, it operates in multiple countries, offering a convenient way for customers to order food through its website or mobile app. The platform allows users to browse menus, read reviews, and place orders for delivery or pickup, providing real-time tracking of the delivery process. With its user-friendly interface and wide variety of cuisine options, Foodpanda has become a go-to solution for both customers seeking convenience and restaurants looking to expand their reach in the digital market.
